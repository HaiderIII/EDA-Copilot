# Questions to Ask the Interviewer

Asking good questions shows your interest and critical thinking.

---

## About the Team and Daily Work

| Question | Why Ask It |
|----------|------------|
| "What does a typical day look like for a CAD engineer on your team?" | Understand the actual work |
| "What are the main EDA tools the team uses?" | Know if it's Cadence, Synopsys, etc. |
| "How does the CAD team collaborate with analog/RF designers?" | Understand the dynamics |
| "What's the ratio of supporting existing tools vs developing new solutions?" | Maintenance vs innovation |

---

## About Projects and Technologies

| Question | Why Ask It |
|----------|------------|
| "What are the biggest automation challenges the team is currently facing?" | Shows you want to solve problems |
| "How is AI/LLM being explored or used in the CAD workflow today?" | Directly related to job posting |
| "What programming languages are most used? SKILL, Python, Perl?" | Know where to focus |
| "How do you handle version control for CAD scripts and methodologies?" | Shows interest in best practices |

---

## About Growth and Learning

| Question | Why Ask It |
|----------|------------|
| "What does success look like in this role after 6 months? After 1 year?" | Understand expectations |
| "Are there opportunities to learn from other CAD teams within Apple?" | Shows ambition |
| "How does the team stay updated with new EDA vendor features?" | Technology watch |

---

## About Culture

| Question | Why Ask It |
|----------|------------|
| "What do you enjoy most about working on this team?" | Personal question, builds rapport |
| "How does the team handle urgent issues during tape-out?" | Understand stress/deadlines |
| "Is there flexibility for remote work or is it fully on-site in Munich?" | Important practical question |

---

## Questions to AVOID

| Don't Ask | Why |
|-----------|-----|
| "What does Apple do?" | You should already know |
| "How much is the salary?" | Wait for the offer |
| "How many vacation days?" | Too early |
| "Did I get the job?" | Never ask this |

---

## Top 3 Recommended Questions

```
1. "What are the main challenges the CAD team is trying to
    solve this year, and how would this role contribute?"

2. "How is the team exploring AI/LLM integration for
    automation, and what's the vision for the next few years?"

3. "What qualities make someone successful in this role?"
```

These questions show:
- You think about problems to solve (not just a job)
- You're interested in AI (as mentioned in the posting)
- You want to succeed and understand expectations
